History

Futuristic Grow is the creator of Toki Sounds (Brazil) and are part of the labels Ovni Records(France) and Azimov Records (India).

Project made by Henrique Jardim, from Minas Gerais, who, since 2011, play at the pick ups and which has international shows in u story. Your song breaks away from the proposal of recent psy-trance and gives to floors a visionary and exotic flow. The objective is always make you dance. Psychedelic trance with a lot of jazz and freestyle forms of music.
