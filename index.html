History

Futuristic Grow is the creator of Toki Sounds (Brazil) and are part of the labels Ovni Records(France) and Azimov Records (India).

Project made by Henrique Jardim, from Minas Gerais, who, since 2011 has been playing as a DJ and in 2018 started to produce your own musics. Henrique has international shows in your carreer. Your style breaks away from the proposal of recent psy-trance and delivery to the floors a visionary and exotic flow. The goal is always make you dance. Psychedelic Trance with a lot of jazz and freestyle forms of music. U can expect something new always when u are listening to Futuristic Grow.

To book Futuristic Grow for u party, write to futuristic@toki.life or scroll down and complete the formulary. Last Release: https://futuristicgrow.bandcamp.com/
